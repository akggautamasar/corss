Telegram Bot Configuration

Get your bot token from @BotFather
Get your channel ID from @username_to_id_bot
This will be displayed in your storage interface

Important Instructions

  1. Make sure your bot is added as an admin to your storage channel
  2. Give your bot the following permissions in the channel:
    • Can post messages
    • Can edit messages
    • Can delete messages
  3. Keep your bot token secure and never share it
